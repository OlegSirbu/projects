Frequently asked questions

  • What is the cost of joining?

    Cooperation with DAVINCI is free of charge. No fees. No commission. No contracts.

  • How can be such a service free of charge?

    Automation. DAVINCI has no branches and is only available online. This can save many of costs associated with a traditional travel agency

  • How will i receive hotel offers from DAVINCI?

    You will see all hotel offers in your profile and you will get email notification as well.

  • How does it work with cancellation policy?

    Hotels will insert their own cancellation policies.

  • How do payments work?

    All payments will be processed by DAVINCI. Final payment is always settled after you upload your rooming list in platform.

  • How are the invoices paid?

    You can pay via bank transfer or you can buy credits in our platform.
